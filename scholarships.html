    Details will be published as they become available.


    ALSA Equity Scholarship

    This scholarship is designed to recognise and assist students who have a demonstrated equity circumstance affecting their ability to study law (including, but not limited to, gender, rural/regional location, extraordinary family commitments, sexuality, ethnicity, poverty, disability, or any other relevant circumstance.)

    3 scholarships are available, each of $500 value.

    Applications are open now


    ALSA Community Involvement Scholarship

    This scholarship is designed to recognise and assist students who have demonstrated a commitment to serving and improving their local community, while studying their degree. This scholarship has an emphasis on Australian programs, as well as programs operating on a local scale and/or in regional areas.

    3 scholarships are available, each of $500 value.

    Applications will open again in 2017


    Rose Lawyers Scholarship Program

    At Rose lawyers, we know our future depends on people like you. That's because those who strive for excellence in their studies often go on to achieve great things in law. And it's that kind of passionate person we love to encourage. To help nurture. And to support through their studies.

    So every year, we offer a $2,000 scholarship to law students. The scholarship, awarded for initiative and forward-thinking, helps students afford the tools they need to excel.

    Applications are open now, and close December 30, 2017. For more details or to apply for this scholarship, visit their website.


    John Koowarta Reconciliation Law Scholarship

    The John Koowarta Reconciliation Law Scholarship is available to Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students enrolled in an approved course of study that is a prerequisite to admission as a legal practitioner in any Australian jurisdiction at or through an Australian tertiary institution.

    Applications for the 2017 Scholarship round are now closed.

    If you would like to be notified when the 2018 round opens, please send an email to koowarta@lawcouncil.asn.au.
